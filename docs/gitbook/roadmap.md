# Roadmap

phases, not dates. the project is one person on one keyboard: things ship when they're ready, and this page says so when they're live. this mirrors [/roadmap](https://outlaws-of-loxley.vercel.app/roadmap) on the site.

## 01 · the greenwood (testnet): live now

- 2,000 outlaws with on-chain stats, weapons and rarity, live on robinhood chain testnet
- mint open with micro test prices, 50 LAWS airdropped per mint so you can fight
- signed duels with elo, permadeath streaks, and the arrow flying across the arena 🏹
- the gallows: dead outlaws hang until someone pays to cut them down
- peer-to-peer marketplace with listing protection in duels

## 02 · the banner goes up: building

- name, handles and domain locked in (x, telegram, discord)
- docs handbook (you're reading it), trust page, contract addresses
- public github repo
- community channels open, first outlaws face off in the arena

## 03 · mainnet on robinhood chain: planned

- $LAWS token launch: fixed supply, LP seeded and burned, team tokens vested
- real mint with founder perks for the first 100
- contracts verified on blockscout, ownership renounced on the token
- keeper bots pegging fight and cut-down costs to usd
- eth or LAWS fights via the currency router

## 04 · deeper into the forest: planned

- staking: lock your outlaws, earn sLAWS by rank and rarity
- tournaments and bounty boards
- whatever the outlaws vote for next

---

no item above is a promise with a date attached, and the game that's live today doesn't depend on any of it shipping. if phase 01 is all that ever exists, the arrows still fly.
