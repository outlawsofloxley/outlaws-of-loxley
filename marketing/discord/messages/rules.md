# House rules

We keep it chill in here. Read once, behave forever.

**1. No scams. No shills.**
Mods never DM first. Anyone DMing you with "support", a "free mint", an "official airdrop", or a wallet-connect prompt is trying to drain you. Block, report, move on. AutoMod nukes the obvious links on sight.

**2. Be civil.**
Keep trash-talk in **#strategy** or **#general**, not at people directly. No racism, no slurs, no targeted harassment. Spoiler tag anything edgy.

**3. Don't shill other tokens here.**
We don't mind you being multi-project — we do mind your charts cluttering our channels. Take it to a DM with whoever wants it.

**4. Help requests go in #help.**
Mods triage from there. Posting the same question across five channels slows everyone down.

**5. Bug reports go in #bug-reports.**
Repro steps, expected vs actual, your wallet, tx hash if relevant. Without those we can't help.

**6. Your seed phrase is yours alone.**
We never ask. We never need it. Anyone DMing you for one is impersonating staff. Screenshot it, post it in #help, and we'll ban them.

**7. Mods have final say.**
We try to be reasonable. If you get warned, take it on the chin. If you disagree, open a thread in #help — don't argue it in public.
