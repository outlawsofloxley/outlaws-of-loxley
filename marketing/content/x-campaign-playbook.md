## 4. Risk notes

**Shadow-ban triggers (X 2026 algo):**
- 3+ hashtags/post = spam filter
- 2+ cashtags stacked consistently = "ticker spam" flag (new post-Smart-Cashtags GA)
- Same reply text within 7 days = duplicate-content pen
- 50+ posts/hour = velocity ban (24-48h)
- Mass-reply same KOL = "reply guy" demotion (7-14d)
- Recovery: minor 2-5d, medium 7-14d, serious 30+. Source: [Sorsa](https://api.sorsa.io/blog/twitter-shadowban-test), [Mozedia](https://www.mozedia.com/how-to-fix-shadowban-on-x-twitter/).

**Reply-guy ratio:**
- Max 1 reply/KOL/48h.
- Max 3 replies/KOL/30d unless they reply back (then loosens).
- If muted: drop them 60d. The mute signal propagates to other KOLs' feeds.

**Best times to reply (UTC, May 2026):**
- **Primary:** 13:00-16:00 UTC (US AM / EU PM) — heaviest crypto-TL activity.
- **Secondary:** 00:00-03:00 UTC (Asia evening) — thinner replies = higher visibility.
- **Best day:** Wed (+17% vs weekly avg). Tue-Thu strongest. Avoid Sat 12:00-18:00 UTC (worst).
- **Velocity:** X 2026 heavily weights first-15-min engagement. Reply <10 min after KOL posts or skip.
- Sources: [Buffer 2026](https://buffer.com/resources/best-time-to-post-on-twitter-x/), [Sprout Social 2026](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/best-times-to-post-on-twitter/).
