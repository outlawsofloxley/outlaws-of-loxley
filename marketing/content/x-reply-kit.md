# X reply kit — tactical templates

Companion to `x-campaign-playbook.md` (KOL list + cashtag/hashtag kit + when-to-reply taxonomy).
This file is the ACTUAL words to type, keyed to common KOL post archetypes.

## Operating rules

- **Never lead with the link.** X throttles replies that contain external links. Drop the link in a SECOND reply under your own first one (chain), or say "pinned" / "in bio". The reply still pulls profile clicks even without a link.
- **One reply per KOL per 48h.** Reply-spamming the same account = mute/block + algorithmic muting of all your future replies under them.
- **Reply within 10 min of the KOL's post.** First-page replies get 95% of the impressions; replies that land 30 min late are dead.
- **Add value first, hook second.** A reply that's pure shill gets quote-clowned. Even one true sentence about the parent post buys the right to add the hook.
- **Mix cashtags sparingly.** 1-2 max in a reply (e.g. `$BRAWL` `$ETH`). 3+ = algorithmic shadow-pen.
- **No hashtags in replies.** Hashtags work in own-posts, not replies. They make replies look bot-y.
- **Read the room.** If the KOL is venting about a bug / loss / personal stuff, DON'T reply with a hook. Skip. Wait for their next post.

## Link-drop strategy (for replies that need a link)

X penalises replies with external links. Workarounds in priority order:

1. **Chain reply** — Top reply has the substance (no link). Reply to your own reply with `"site: baseicbrawlers.com"` as a 2nd msg. The link reply gets de-amped but the parent stays visible.
2. **Pinned tweet** — Pin a single tweet with the site + CA + chart. Reference "see pinned" in replies. Drives profile clicks instead.
3. **Bio link** — Make sure the @BASEicBrawlers bio carries `baseicbrawlers.com`. "in bio" is a known X-native phrase, no penalty.
4. **Quote tweet** — If you want the link in front of the KOL's audience, quote-tweet rather than reply. Quote tweets show on your followers' feeds AND on the link-click path; replies don't.
