🎮 HOW IT WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. mint a brawler at /mint. 6 tiers $20 to $50, paid in ETH or USDC. founders are token IDs 1-100.
2. enter the arena. fight cost ~$1 in $BRAWL or ETH.
3. win = +ELO + share of the pot. lose = -ELO. 3 losses in a row = NFT dies.
4. resurrect with ETH ($100 base, scales by rarity + wins, capped at $500). founders get one free.
